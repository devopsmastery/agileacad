SAFe Trainings

SAFe (Scaled Agile Framework for Enterprises) is one of the leading frameworks used in Scaling Agile projects/programs.

The Scaled Agile Framework® (SAFe) is a scaling method from Scaled Academy specifically targeted towards large-scale Agile Programs for acheiving Business Agility. Understanding and Application of Lean/Agile principles and practices across all levels of an enterprise is crucial to acheive agility across the board. The SAFe® framework effortlessly synchronizes the alignment, collaboration, and delivery of multiple agile teams, leading the way to enterprise success.

The latest version of SAFe 5.0, promises to bring in full business agility with significant advances in strategy, execution, and leadership competencies that will help individuals and organizations deliver innovative business solutions and supersede the competition.

Why SAFe is important today?

Recent years have seen Agile practices flow out of IT and into various business departments, leading to Agile Engineering, Agile Human Resources and Agile Marketing organizations, among others. This expansion is likely to continue at an even faster pace in 2020.

Enterprise Agile is reshaping the way enterprises do businessand Agile is the best way anyone has found to create products that serve customers and drive business value. The principles of constant feedback and rapid iteration are just as valid at the enterprise scale as they are for smaller organizations.

SAFe is rightly placed to provide enterprises with a defined framework and measuring business agility which has been proven over many years of evolution.

SAFe 5.0's focus on business agility brings the much needed aligment between business and technology leaders and provides an oppurtinity to business and management leaders to apply lean and Agile practices to remains competitive and innovative.
